Our Services

Regular Cleaning

Our standard cleaning service covers all main areas of your home on a scheduled basis.

  • Vacuuming and mopping all floors
  • Dusting surfaces, shelves, and fixtures
  • Bathroom and kitchen cleaning
  • Emptying bins and general tidying

Deep Cleaning

A thorough, top-to-bottom clean for homes that need extra attention or periodic intensive care.

  • Everything in Regular Cleaning
  • Inside oven, fridge, and appliances
  • Grout and tile scrubbing
  • Behind and under furniture
  • Window sills and skirting boards

Move-In / Move-Out

Perfect when transitioning between homes — we ensure the property is spotless for new occupants.

Removal & Storage

Combined cleaning and removal service to help you clear and clean simultaneously.

Booking a Service

Follow these simple steps to schedule your cleaning:

  • Step 1: Visit our homepage and click "Get a free quote"
  • Step 2: Select your service type and preferred date/time
  • Step 3: Enter your address and any special instructions
  • Step 4: Confirm your booking — you'll receive an email confirmation
  • Step 5: Our team arrives on time, fully equipped

You can also call us directly or send us a message via the Contact page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be home during the cleaning? ▼
No — many clients provide a key or access code. Our cleaners are fully vetted and insured, so you can trust them to clean while you're away.
What cleaning products do you use? ▼
We use eco-friendly, non-toxic products that are safe for children and pets. If you have specific allergies or preferences, let us know when booking.
How do I cancel or reschedule? ▼
You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Contact us via the Contact page or by phone.
What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning? ▼
We have a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If anything was missed, simply contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to address it free of charge.
Are your cleaners insured? ▼
Yes. All GlanzDrive cleaning professionals are fully insured and have passed comprehensive background checks before joining our team.
Do you offer recurring cleaning plans? ▼
Absolutely! We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly plans with exclusive discounts of up to 20% compared to one-time bookings.
